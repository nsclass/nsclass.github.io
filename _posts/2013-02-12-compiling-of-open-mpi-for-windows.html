When you compile OpenMPI on Windows, you should define the following preprocessor.

OMPI_IMPORTS
OPAL_IMPORTS
ORTE_IMPORTS

Example)
c:\>mpicxx test.cpp -DOMPI_IMPORTS -DOPAL_IMPORTS -DORTE_IMPORTS
